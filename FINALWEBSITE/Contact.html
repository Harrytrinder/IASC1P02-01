Boxing Saftey

Boxing goes to a great deal of lengths to protect its athletes, along with the required head gear, mouth guard and gloves, boxers are also required to have a pre and post medicals.

At FIGHT4KNOWLEDGE we believe that all forms of sport activity should be conducted in a safe, supervised and unique manner. All trainers are selected, trained and assessed, to be able to deliver the best experience in sport and education.

At the end of the two month period their is an opportunity for participants to take part in an organised event where they can show the technical skills they have acquired from the program
