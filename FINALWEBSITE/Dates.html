Fight 4 Knowledge

Every 2 Months Starting January 2018

Breakdown

8 weeks, 16 sessions - 2 groups

Group 1 – 13-18yrs

Group 2 – 18yrs+

Group Max = 30 people

Coaches = 1 per 10 people (boxing and dance)

Teachers = 1 per lesson

When

Group 1

Group 1 = Sunday Mornings 9-11AM

Group 1 = Wednesday, 7-9PM

Group 2

Group 2 = Saturday Mornings 9-11AM

Group 2 = Tuesday, 7-9PM

Twice a week, every week for two months a session of boxing and dance will commence, in separate places which will be circulated through a 2 hour session

First hour = English teaching – Talking and communicating personal goals, aspirations and achievements to increase the motivation and vision of those who take part – A process to accomplish these goals will be offered to the students to formulate an action plan for personal external achievements, outside of the sessions

Second hour = Training - Physical excursion to increase heart rate and blood flow - Set programs for Boxing and Dance

What You Will Gain

Progress in understanding and speaking of the English language

Sport Participation in Dance or Boxing training

Physical appearance and Mental wellbeing

Opportunity to compete and perform
