Products

We make elegant furniture and furnishings for every room in the home, from living and dining rooms to hallways, kitchens, bedrooms, kids’ rooms, home offices and bathrooms.



Our latest collection includes fresh new furniture designs, more clever storage solutions and an even bigger selection of beautiful fabrics and wallpapers than ever before.

We offer modular designs that allow you to create the perfect configuration for your room.
Our affordable bespoke element gives you the freedom to specify fabrics, paint colours, handles and knobs from within our collection so the furniture you order suits your scheme perfectly. We will even alter the size of standard products where we can.

Launched in 1983, we are a UK company celebrating 32 proud years of British manufacture. 
Every order is custom made, so delivery usually takes 4 to 6 weeks, but check with our team if your order’s urgent: we always try to help.
Customer satisfaction is our priority; we strive to offer excellent service and quality and all products come with The Dormy House guarantee.
